About Big2Projects

Building a continental agri-movement to be a force in sustainable agricultural production amidst the global climate change crisis

Who We Are

Big2Projects Organization is a for-profit agricultural development enterprise established in 2023, registered in Malawi. We are a leading force in sustainable agriculture, recognized for our commitment to high-quality crop production and dedication to cultivating skilled, resilient farmers who drive positive change within their communities.

Our approach goes beyond traditional farming - we empower youth with climate-smart agricultural practices, provide expert consultation services, undertake agricultural commercialization, and encourage natural resource management through environmental transformation programs.

Farmer's Clinic Market

Your trusted partner in agricultural excellence and farmer support

Comprehensive Agricultural Support Hub

The Farmer's Clinic Market serves as both a physical hub and knowledge center, providing end-to-end support for farmers at every stage of their agricultural journey.

  • Farmer Advisory & Extension Support
  • Input Supply and Produce Marketing
  • Consultancy and Agribusiness Training
  • Organic Fertilizer Production
  • Market Linkages for Cooperatives
  • Climate-Smart Farming Techniques
  • Crop Production Management
  • Soil Health Assessment
